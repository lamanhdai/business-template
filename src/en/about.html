About

Historical development

NONG SAN VIET (VIET ACC) is a leading company in the field of Trading Agricultural commodities, Premix and Feed additives used in the production of livestock and aqua in Vietnam.

VIET ACC has gradually established its position in the market by becoming the exclusive distributor of major brands in the world such as DSM, Diamond V, Olmix. ..

With a strong potential, skilled staff and experience in the livestock industry is a solid foundation for VIET ACC to develop sustainably and permanently.
